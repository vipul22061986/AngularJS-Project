      About We Music

      The music videos shown here are from a playlist of all Is We Ting's favorite Caribbean hits. It comprises of some of the hits and not so popular songs from the different Caribbean islands. It contains a range of genres; reggae, dancehall, soca, kompa, reggaeton, zouk and more, all for you to enjoy. We at Is We Ting are always looking to hear about new Caribbean music and artists so please reach out to us and let us know. We would love to include some of your favorites in this playlist as well.
